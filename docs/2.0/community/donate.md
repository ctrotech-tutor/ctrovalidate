## Why Sponsor?

- **Infrastructure**: Help cover the costs of our documentation hosting and CI/CD pipelines.
- **Maintenance**: Ensure that bugs are fixed quickly and the library stays compatible with modern browsers.
- **New Features**: Accelerate the development of advanced rules, framework adapters, and tooling.
- **Accessibility**: Support our mission to make every form on the web accessible by default.
